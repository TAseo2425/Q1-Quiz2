Miraculous: Tales of Ladybug and Cat Noir


A French animation that is about two students, Marinette Dupain-Cheng and Adrien Agreste, that are normal students in the day but are heroes at night. They fight that civilians that are turned into villians through akumatization. These two were given magical jewelry that grants the wearer the ability to transform and gain powers. These civilians are akumatized by Hawkmoth, who tries to steal their jewels that were given by the former Great Guardian of the Miraculouses, Wang Fu.
